gavel Judgment
For each moment the gate noticed, the L1.5 judge (a cheap LLM) scored whether it was real speech and worth attention, and why. Whether to perk up is an LLM judgment — not something we tune from labels — so your role here is to review the judge: reveal the transcript, see the verdict + reasoning, and record your own take and, most usefully, why. Your reviews inform the judge's prompt, never the trigger weights. This is about attention (worth being aware of / remembering), not interrupting — Worth noticing = attend · Not worth it = noise · Skip = can't tell (garbled). A "not worth it" is not a verdict on the trigger — you may decline for reasons of your own; the note is where that reasoning lives. Transcript text is read live from the local snapshot; it is never stored here.
